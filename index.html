When you create an account, you must register a phone number. This phone number facilitates our Services not only for you but also for other users. Optionally, you may append additional details to your account, such as a profile name and picture, all of which are safeguarded by end-to-end encryption.

Messages

Rest assured, we cannot decrypt or access the content of your messages or calls. The app queues end-to-end encrypted messages on its servers for devices that may be temporarily offline (for instance, if a phone's battery depletes). Your message history is retained solely on your devices.

Contacts

Our app may, with your permission, uncover which contacts in your address book are also Signal users, employing a service meticulously designed to protect your contacts' privacy. Information from your device's contacts may be cryptographically hashed and sent to the server to identify registered contacts.
